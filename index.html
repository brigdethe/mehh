Our mission is to transform local okada business.
We connect skilled riders to a trusted system—bringing safe, affordable transport to the communities that need it most.
In rural Ghana, unregulated motorbike taxis make transport unsafe and unreliable.
ORDA Rides formalizes okada services by connecting skilled riders to a trusted system communities can rely on.
By formalizing local transport, we generate work for riders and businesses alike while proviing a safe transport service.
